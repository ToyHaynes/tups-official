Privacy Policy for Tups Pro

Last Updated: December 12, 2024

1. Information We Collect

Tups Pro is designed to respect your privacy. We collect minimal information to provide and improve our services:

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account or make purchases, we may collect your email address and payment information through our secure payment processor.
  • App Settings: Your app preferences and settings are stored locally on your device.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Analytics: We collect anonymized usage data to improve app performance and features using Firebase Analytics.
  • Crash Reports: We collect crash logs to identify and fix technical issues using Firebase Crashlytics.
  • Device Information: Basic device information (OS version, device model) for compatibility purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Tups Pro app
  • Process subscription payments and manage your account
  • Improve app performance and fix technical issues
  • Develop new features and enhancements
  • Send important service updates and notifications

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • Service Providers: We work with trusted service providers (RevenueCat for subscriptions, Firebase for analytics) who assist in app operations.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and users' safety.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols
  • Payment processing is handled by certified, PCI-compliant payment processors
  • We regularly review and update our security practices

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we have about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics collection in app settings

6. Children's Privacy

Tups Pro is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

7. International Users

Tups Pro is available globally. If you use our app from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and stored in the United States where our servers are located.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Third-Party Services

Tups Pro integrates with the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: Handles subscription management and payments. View RevenueCat's Privacy Policy
  • Firebase (Google): Provides analytics and crash reporting. View Google's Privacy Policy

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@tupsapp.com
App: Tups Pro
Developer: TUPS Team

We will respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 30 days.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of December 12, 2025, and applies to all users of the Tups Pro mobile application.
