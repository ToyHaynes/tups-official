üîÑ My subscription isn't activating after purchase
Go to the app's Settings screen and tap "Restore Purchases". This will sync your subscription with our servers. If the issue persists, try closing the app completely and reopening it. Make sure you're logged in with the same Apple ID or Google account you used for the purchase.
üîä I can't hear any sounds from the app
Check these settings: 1) Ensure your device is not on silent mode, 2) Increase your device volume, 3) Check if other apps play sound normally, 4) In iOS, go to Settings > Sounds & Haptics and adjust the Ringer and Alerts volume, 5) Force quit the app and restart it.
üí∞ How do I cancel my subscription?
iOS: Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > TUPS Pro > Cancel Subscription
Android: Open Google Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions > TUPS Pro > Cancel Subscription
Note: Uninstalling the app does not cancel your subscription.
üîÑ How do I request a refund?
Refunds are processed directly through the App Store or Google Play Store:
iOS: Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID
Android: Visit play.google.com/store/account/orderhistory and request a refund
Please note that we cannot process refunds directly as all payments are handled by Apple/Google.
üì± The app is crashing or running slowly
Try these troubleshooting steps: 1) Update to the latest version of TUPS Pro from the App Store/Play Store, 2) Restart your device, 3) Ensure you have at least 100MB of free storage space, 4) Close other apps running in the background, 5) If the problem persists, contact us with your device model and iOS/Android version.

üìã Before Contacting Support

Please have this information ready:

  • Your device model (e.g., iPhone 14, Samsung Galaxy S23)
  • Operating system version (e.g., iOS 17.0, Android 14)
  • TUPS Pro app version (found in Settings > About)
  • Screenshots or screen recordings of the issue (if applicable)
  • Detailed description of what you were doing when the problem occurred
  • Steps you've already tried to resolve the issue
